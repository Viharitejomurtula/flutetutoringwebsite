Comprehensive Flute Education

From the first note to advanced techniques, here's what lessons include:

  • Lessons for All Skill Levels: From absolute beginners learning their first notes to advanced players looking to refine their technique.
  • Building a Strong Foundation: A core focus on fundamental skills like proper tone production, posture, breathing, and music theory.
  • Skill Sharpening & Performance: Guidance on advanced techniques, repertoire development, and performance coaching.
  • Lesson Structure: Lessons are structured as four 30-minute classes per month, held once a week, for a monthly rate of $100. More intensive schedules can be accommodated upon request.
  • Flexible Formats: To accommodate your needs, classes can be conducted in a hybrid manner (both in-person and virtual) or held completely online.
